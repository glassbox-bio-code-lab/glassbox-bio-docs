# Privacy Policy

This page rewrites the current privacy-policy source material into documentation form. It summarizes what Glassbox says it collects, why it collects it, how it may share it, and what rights users should expect.

**Effective date:** February 11, 2026

## Information collected

The current policy material describes these main data categories:

- email addresses and account information
- payment identifiers and transaction IDs
- target submissions and other materials provided for audit services
- personal and professional contact information
- communications such as inquiries, support requests, and feedback
- technical website data such as IP address, browser, device, usage, and cookies

## How the information is used

The policy says Glassbox uses collected information to:

- provide, maintain, and improve services
- process transactions and requests
- communicate about services, updates, and support
- send marketing communications where permitted
- analyze usage and improve the user experience
- detect technical issues and security threats
- comply with legal obligations and enforce terms

## Sharing and disclosure

The source policy says Glassbox does not sell personal information. It may share data in limited cases such as:

- service providers and business partners operating under confidentiality obligations
- Stripe for payment processing
- DocuSign for document-signing workflows
- legal process, government request, or protection of rights and safety
- business-transfer events such as merger or acquisition
- explicit user consent

## Security posture

The privacy material states that Glassbox uses technical and organizational safeguards such as:

- encryption in transit and at rest
- authentication and access controls
- security reviews and updates
- employee training on data protection

It also states the usual limitation that no online system can guarantee absolute security.

## User rights and requests

The direct privacy material says users may have rights, depending on jurisdiction, to:

- access their information
- correct inaccurate information
- request deletion or anonymization
- opt out of certain marketing or processing uses

## International transfers and children

The policy source also states that:

- information may be transferred across countries with appropriate safeguards
- the services are not intended for children under 18
