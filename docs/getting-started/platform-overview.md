# Platform Overview

Glassbox Bio should be explained as a platform with distinct product surfaces:

- **Computational Safety Diligence** for core analysis and outputs
- **PreFlight UI** for pre-submission verification and workflow orchestration
- **Add-Ons** for optional, specialized capability layers

## Why this structure matters

Users should be able to answer three questions quickly:

1. What surface am I using?
2. What stage of the workflow am I in?
3. Which outputs should I trust and act on?

## Product taxonomy

### Core product
Computational Safety Diligence is the primary analysis and reporting surface.

### Workflow product
PreFlight UI is the readiness and verification surface before submission.

### Extension products
Add-Ons are optional capability layers that extend the workflow without being mistaken for internal core modules.
